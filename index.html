0. Preamble

In the following document...

... The terms 'approved by the Councils', 'passed by the Councils', 'decided by the Councils' are used to refer to any motion drafted by 'Council' and approved by 'High Council'.

... The phrase 'The position A is appointed by B' is used to indicate that a certain department or person <B> has total or shared control on the people who fill a specific role or position <A>.

R.K.R. Title 1 ー Structure

1 RKR § 01 ー High Council

( a ) Position of High Council ー
'High Council' is the highest institution.
( b ) Powers of High Council ー
    ( 1 ) 'High Council' can 'veto' any 'motion'.
    ( 2 ) 'High Council' is the only institution that can pass a legislation.
( c ) High Council Passing of Motions ー
    ( 1 ) 'High Council' shall reach a consensus before passing a legislation.

1 RKR § 02 ー Council

( a ) Generally ー
'Council' shall be in charge of writing legislation.
( b ) Council Passing of Motions ー
'Council' shall pass motions through majority vote.

1 RKR § 04 ー Special powers and obligations of the Titles Department

( a ) Buffs ー
'Council' vests, with authority borrowed from 'High Council', in the 'Titles' Department the power to choose buff times.

( b ) Shifts ー
'Council' requires the appointed 'Leaders' to ensure the members of the 'Titles Department' work 'title giving shifts'.

R.K.R. Title 2 ー Alliances

2 RKR § 01 ー Leadership

( a ) Definition of Alliance Leadership ー
    ( 1 ) Generally, Alliance Leaderships are a special type of 'Executive Departments' that are under 'High Council', but behave as if they were under 'Council'.
    ( 2 ) They have a 'Leader'.
    ( 3 ) Their leader is appointed by 'High Council'.
    ( 4 ) Members are appointed by the 'Leader'.
        ( 4-a ) The 'Leader' is commonly called 'R5' and the members are commonly called 'R4'.
