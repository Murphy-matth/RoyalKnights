“RKR„ Title 1 ー Structure

1 RKR § 01 ー High Council

( a ) Position of High Council ー
'High Council' is the highest institution.

( b ) Powers of High Council ー
    ( 1 ) 'High Council' can 'veto' any 'motion'.

    ( 2 ) 'High Council' is the only institution that can pass a legislation.

( c ) High Council Passing of Motions ー
    ( 1 ) 'High Council' shall reach a consensus before passing a legislation.

1 RKR § 02 ー Council

( a ) Generally ー
'Council' shall be in charge of writing legislation.

( b ) Council Passing of Motions ー
'Council' shall pass motions through majority vote.

( c ) Distribution of Council ー
The Ri alliance shall have 1 'Council seat', the alliances, 'RA', 'RX', 'RK', 'RP' and 'RR' shall have 2, unless otherwise stated by 'High Council'.
( d ) Council Emergency Motion ー
'Council' may pass a motion to zero a user if a city hit has occured with solely 3 votes.

1 RKR § 04 ー Special powers and obligations of the Titles Department

( a ) Buffs ー
'Council' vests, with authority borrowed from 'High Council', in the 'Titles' Department the power to choose buff times.

( b ) Shifts ー
'Council' requires the appointed 'Leaders' to ensure the members of the 'Titles Department' work 'title giving shifts'.

“RKR„ Title 2 ー Alliances

2 RKR § 01 ー Definitions

( a ) Definition of Alliance Leadership ー

    ( 1 ) Generally, Alliance Leaderships are a special type of 'Executive Departments' that are under 'High Council', but behave as if they were under 'Council'.

    ( 2 ) Alliance Leaderships have a 'Leader'.

    ( 3 ) An Alliance Leadership's 'Leader' is appointed by 'High Council'.

    ( 4 ) Members (R4s) are appointed by the 'Leader'.
        ( 4-a ) The 'Leader' is commonly called 'R5' and the members are commonly called 'R4'.

( b ) Definition of Official Alliances ー

    ( 1 ) 'Official Alliances' are alliances that have a Leadership 'Executive Branch' tied to them.

    ( 2 ) 'Official Alliances' have two constituents, 'Leadership' which is an Alliance Leadership Executive Branch tied to the alliance and members, who are decided by the alliance's tied 'Leadership'.

2 RKR § 02 ー Obligations

( a ) Obligations of Alliances Leadership ー

    ( 1 ) It is mandatory that Alliance Leadership keep track of who their members are.

    ( 2 ) It is mandatory that Alliance Leadership works to have the resources necessary to build flags or fortresses prescribed by the 'Councils'.

( b ) Position of Alliance Members ー

Alliance members are directly under their respective Leadership.

“RKR„ Title 3 ー Offenses

3 RKR § 01 ー Major Offenses

( a ) City Hits ー
    ( 1 ) Generally, city hits are not allowed except exceptions stated in 3 RKR § 01{a-3}.

    ( 2 ) The punishment for any who City Hits shall be a zero.

    ( 3 ) Exceptions to 3 RKR § 01{a-3} are:

  • The 'City Hall Level' of the victim was under 8
  • The 'City hit' was prescribed by a 'Council Emergency Motion'.
  • The 'City Hit' was prescribed by 'High Council'
